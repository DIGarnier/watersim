## What this says the next optimizations must attack

- **BH path: the force traversal is ~75–82 % of the step** (11.7 of 14.3 ms
  at 24k). Anything that reduces traversal *frequency* (multiple time
  stepping, stage 14) or traversal *depth* (θ, stage 13) attacks the
  dominant term directly.
- **Grid paths: the contact solver is ~60–73 % of the step** (2.1–2.4 of
  2.9–3.5 ms at 24k). The solver runs a fixed 4 iterations; iteration count ×
  relaxation is the lever (stage 15).
- The force pass is the second-largest grid-path term (e.g. 329 µs of
  1118 µs at 12k verlet) — multiple time stepping applies there too.
